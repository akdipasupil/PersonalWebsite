Guitar Piano Hero Festival

For this project, I tried to recreate a game similar to guitar hero. There are five different buttons lined up with each other, and each button plays a different tone, and lights up the corresponding light on the right side. There are also two buttons on the side, the left one plays back the tones in the order that the user played them in. The right button clears the stored tones and allows for the player to begin playing once again.
